Pathway Clash

A Lord of the Mysteries Card Game

What is this?

A blind card battler based on the 22 Pathways from Lord of the Mysteries. Each pathway has unique strengths and weaknesses against others, creating a strategic rock-paper-scissors style game.

How to Play

  • Draw 3 cards from the 22 pathways each round
  • Select a card to play against your opponent
  • Win, lose, or draw based on pathway matchups
  • Deal damage when you win - stronger matchups deal more!
  • First to 0 HP loses the match

Tips

  • Hover over cards to see their flavor text
  • Watch the battle log to see matchup results and damage
  • Rainbow names indicate powerful "Big Hit" pathways
  • Draws double the damage multiplier for the next round!
  • Press ? anytime during the game to view this screen
